Ingredients:
	- 1lb ground turkey
	- 1 can tomato sauce
	- 1 can diced tomatoes
	- 1 can pinto beans
	- 1 can diced green chili peppers
	- 1 packet taco seasoning

Directions:
	1. In a saute pan, brown the ground turkey

	2. In a large pot, combine browned turkey and all other ingredients. Do not drain any of the canned ingredients. Stir to combine.

	3. Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce heat to simmer for 30 minutes
